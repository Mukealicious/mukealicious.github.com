Mikey Who?

I am a purpose driven designer with a passion for products. A realigner not a redesigner. I don't like making things pretty, I like making them better.

I've always thought of myself to have a conflicted personality. Right brain vs left brain. Artist vs accountant. I'm an artist in the sense that I love ideas and doing things in a new way. I work best on the outer edges, not the status quo. Ideas really inspire and motivate me to do good work. On the other side, I'm an accountant type in the sense that everything has to have a place. There must be purpose behind every product and design decision. You could say, in terms of design, the books have to balance.

I learned web design, as well as every other form of design I could sink my teeth into, back in high school. Web design never fully hooked me because of the lack of control that was possible at that time visually. So, for that reason, I gravitated toward print design.

I started my professional career as a designer about 5 years ago in the apparel industry. Working as an apparel designer taught me so much about design, and also that I can't stand the traditional retail model. Around a year ago I wrote this article touching on the subject. This was shortly before I decided to take a step back and fully focus on the web.

I love that a small group of peers that work in a very non-digital industry, but have an understanding of digital, can team up and turn that industry on it's head. That's the power of the web, not just what we're seeing in the "tech" and surrounding industries.
